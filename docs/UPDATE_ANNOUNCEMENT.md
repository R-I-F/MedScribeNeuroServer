# Update Announcement (WhatsApp)

Copy the message below and send it to the group. The single asterisks show as *bold* in WhatsApp.

---

Dear all,

Our app update is now live. Here is a simple summary of what is new and what changed.

*A fresh, easier app*
- A brand new design across the whole app. Cleaner, simpler, and easier to use.
- The app is now fully bilingual. You can switch between English and Arabic any time, and the Arabic view reads right to left correctly, including all the data and charts.

*Now supports 15 departments, not just Neurosurgery*
- The app used to serve Neurosurgery only. It now supports 15 medical and surgical departments: Neurosurgery, Cardiothoracic, General Surgery, Hepatobiliary and Pancreatic, Maxillofacial, Obstetrics and Gynaecology, Ophthalmology, Orthopedics, ENT, Pediatric Surgery, Plastic and Reconstructive, Surgical Oncology, Transplant, Urology, and Vascular.
- Each department comes with its own complete set of diagnoses, procedures, academic lectures, and case questions. All of it was carefully reviewed and prepared in both English and Arabic.
- Everything is grouped by department, so each person sees the surgeries, events, reports, and reference data for their own department only.
- When you sign up, you now choose your department.

*Safer sign up and login*
- New accounts now confirm your email with a 6 digit code before the account is created. This keeps accounts genuine.
- We reviewed and strengthened the security of the whole app (stronger login protection, better privacy, and stricter access so people only see what they should).

*Dashboards for every role*
- Candidates, supervisors, calendar managers, and admins each have their own dashboard built for what they do.
- Dashboards show your surgeries, academic activity, rankings, and clear charts: top procedures, top diagnoses, equipment used, and consumables used.

*For admins*
- Institute admins now see and manage only their own department.
- The super admin can create department based institute admins and calendar managers, and browse the reference data (procedures, diagnoses, and more) for any department.

*Your data is safe*
- The app moved to a new, faster, and more reliable system.
- All of your existing records (surgeries, submissions, approvals, clinical cases, and academic activity) were carefully moved over. Nothing was lost.

If you notice anything odd or have a question, just reply here and we will help.

Thank you,
The Team
